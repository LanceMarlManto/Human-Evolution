Our Educational Mission

This Human Evolution website was created as part of our Empowerment Technology class project. Our goal is to make the fascinating story of human origins accessible and engaging for everyone, combining scientific accuracy with compelling visual design.

Our Mission

To educate and inspire curiosity about human evolution through interactive, visually-rich content that makes complex scientific concepts understandable to all audiences.

Our Vision

We envision a world where everyone understands our shared evolutionary heritage and appreciates how science helps us understand what makes us human.
